1. **Advance Payments** – All advance payments made toward a project are non-refundable, as they secure project commencement and resource allocation.

2. **Milestone Payments** – Payments made upon completion of a milestone are non-refundable, as the work has been delivered and accepted.

3. **Third-Party Expenses** – Any amounts paid toward third-party services, tools, APIs, or infrastructure on behalf of the client are non-refundable.

4. **Valid Refund Scenarios** – In any other valid scenario where a refund is applicable, the refund will be processed within 15 working days from the date of approval.

5. **How to Request** – To request a refund, please contact us at support@embetter.in with your invoice details and reason for the request.

6. **Contact** – For any questions regarding this policy, reach out to us at support@embetter.in.
